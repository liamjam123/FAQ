How to Disable Clutter on Outlook

To disable Clutter, you will need to login to Outlook Online Web Access which you can do HERE
Once you have logged in, right click Clutter on the left hand column and select Clutter settings.
This will take you straight to the window you can see down below.
Now untick the Separate items identified as clutter option and click Save.
This will stop emails classed as low priority from being filtered into the Clutter folder on your Outlook.
This folder will still show in Outlook but by following these steps will become inactive.
